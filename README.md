## fridg3.org
I've transformed this site into my own personal projection, to the point where I can and do use it as a substitute for typical social media. 

I share my life updates via the Feed which simulates a Twitter feed _(some friends post here too sometimes!)_, the Newsletter which is a monthly update on my life and developments _(you can receive this via email automatically by signing up to the newsletter!)_ and the Journal which acts as a blog for projects, events and more recently, big purchases. 

I also share my music releases on the Music page _(as well as streaming platforms)_. You can listen to my music on-site as you browse it, and download any tracks by clicking on the album art in the mini-player while playing.

There are various other things that my website offers: physical merchandise, theme customization, and so much more! Have a browse yourself, and after you visit, go to the Guestbook and leave a nice comment. If you wish to support running my website and keeping it online, you can buy merch, or if you'd like to give me money directly (without giving anything to a third party), you can send me an email and we'll figure something out. The website costs roughly £7 a month to host, and the domain is roughly £25 a year.
